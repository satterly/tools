@satterly I have a situation where Grafana sends an alert to alerta, and continues to do so for every n minuts where the situation fits grafanas criteria for an alert. But alerta doesn't treat it as a new alert and just opens the old expired alert. I have email and SMS sent, triggered by alerta when an alert happens. So when the alert is not removed/deleted alerta and grafana sends an alert I'm not notified. But at the same time I'm interested in the latest alerts, even though they arn't active. Any idea on how to solv my case?
