@graysonhead Concerning the amount of email that can be generated - I've had really good luck with using Logstash to send email. The Alerta Logstash plugin sends all the alerts to Logstash, and then you use the Logstash filter to drop all alerts that have a duplicate count != 0. You wind up with only sending emails that are state changes and not repeats of previous state.
