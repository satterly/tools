I was looking into why we have sent so many alerts after you mentioned it yesterday. The vast majority of these are HeartbeatOK events that come from our cron job to monitor heartbeats:
* * * * * /usr/local/bin/alerta --endpoint-url http://127.0.0.1:8080 heartbeats --alert > /dev/null 2>&1
We have two api servers w/a single postgres backend, so that is effectively 2/minute * ~ 100 hosts sending heartbeats. That explains the large number (in that each of these creates a repeat of the HeartbeatOK event), but I wouldn't have thought our number would have been so unusual to overflow this integer field.
