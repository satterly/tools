Nick Satterly
@satterly
May 21 2019 11:54 UTC
Your application needs to send the heartbeat repeatedly within the timeout interval otherwise it will expire. Expired heartbeats can generate alerts if configured to do so.
And no, the heartbeat will not still work after test.py finishes. You need to schedule test.py to run at regular intervals.
