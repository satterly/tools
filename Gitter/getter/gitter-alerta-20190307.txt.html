The prometheus plugin stores the “silence Id” that is returned in the response from alertmanager in the alert attribute that has been acked in Alerta. The silenceId is then retrieved from alert attributes and used to delete the suppression in alertmanager when an operator un-acks the alert.
https://github.com/alerta/alerta-contrib/blob/master/plugins/prometheus/alerta_prometheus.py
