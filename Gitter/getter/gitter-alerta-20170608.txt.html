@tester22 what i would suggest is that you give every alert type a different event name but then do not use the “correlation” attribute to make them correlate. For example, if you had disk space alerts I’d call them “DiskFull”, “DiskHigh”, “DiskOk” (or something) and then leave it at that. normally I would define a list of events that would correlate (ie. [“DiskFull”, “DiskHigh”, “DiskOk”] in this case) however for your use-case do nothing more.
