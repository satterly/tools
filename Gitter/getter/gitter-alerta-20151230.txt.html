Nick Satterly
@satterly
Dec 30 2015 09:36 UTC
i think sending to different alerta servers using the same mongodb replica set would work but it’s not the way I would suggest. I would use a load-balancer (eg. just HAProxy) in front of multiple alerta servers that used the mongodb replica set. it’s a more common HA web service approach and you wouldn’t have the problem of dealing with multiple post_receive hooks being triggered as well.
