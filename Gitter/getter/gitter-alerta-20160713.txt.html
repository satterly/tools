Jul 13 2016 14:34 UTC
that service drop down is already grouped by environment. i don’t think grouping it additionally by some other attribute would be possible (in angularjs). and i would be against encoding special information into a field. ie. making dots inside a service name mean something. i’ve tried to avoid that where possible which is why there are so many different alert attributes, and the ability to add your own custom ones.
however, i think your idea has merit. there are definitely many different ways the alert data can be aggregated and displayed and the one i’ve developed with the alert UI is only one possibility, and not necessarily the best. certainly not the best for everyone.
i’ve had thoughts and ideas for how a more configurable alerta console might look and feel and i’m happy to receive feedback like this to add to the mix. there are no immediate plans to kick off development of a new one though. i’ll wait to see where angular 2 is going and what the take up and support is like. likewise with bootstrap 4.
