@marwakrouma I think the problem is that the service needs to be tuple or list, not a string.
Looking at the code, it looks like service, correlate and tags are all expected to be lists.
So this will probably work: 
from alertaclient.api import Client
api_request=Client(endpoint=self.endpoint_alerta, key=self.api_key)
api_request.send_alert(customer=self.alert_customer,service=[self.alert_service],environment=self.alert_environment,resource=self.alert_resource,event=self.alert_event,severity=self.alert_severity,text=self.alert_text,attributes=alert_attributes)
